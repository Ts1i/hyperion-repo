Fusion House

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Fusion House emerged from a passion for blending traditional Thai flavors with modern culinary innovations. Our journey began when three friends discovered their shared love for creating unique dining experiences that bridge cultural gaps through food.

Our Food

At Fusion House, we craft dishes that honor Thai culinary traditions while embracing contemporary techniques. Our menu features creative interpretations of classic Thai dishes, using locally-sourced ingredients and authentic Thai spices.


From our signature lemongrass-infused curries to modern takes on pad thai, each dish tells a story of innovation and respect for tradition.

Our People

At Fusion House, we are proud to have an exceptional team that drives our success.

Our CEO, Sarah Chen, leads with vision and passion, inspiring us to reach new heights. Mark Rodriguez, our CTO, brings innovative technology solutions that enhance our culinary experiences.

Apinya Thompson, our Head Chef, infuses every dish with creativity and expertise, making each meal unforgettable. Together, they create a harmonious blend of leadership, innovation, and culinary artistry that defines Fusion House.
